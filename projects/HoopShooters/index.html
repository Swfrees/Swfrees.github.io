HoopShooters

A fast-paced and exciting game where the player has one simple objective, to score as many baskets as possible before the timer runs out. The game takes advantage of UE5's physics engine and vertex math to make the game feel as realistic as possible.

  • Project Type: Arcade basketball game
  • Date: 12/2022 - 01/2023
  • Roles: Game Developer Game Designer Level Designer
  • Tools: Unreal Engine 5 Github Blender


    • The player watches the ball fly through the air at a velocity of 22500 units in hopes of getting their first point.


    The player looks down at the ball to ensure the line tracing math puts the player within grabbing distance of the basketball.


    The player looks to add a third point to their total after releasing the ball at the forward vector of the players camera.


    The player watches as the ball goes through the hoop crossing over the trigger box within the hoop to register scoring another point.


    The player watches on as the ball flies towards the hoop following the impulse from the player releasing the ball.
